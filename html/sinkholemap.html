Tampa Bay-area sinkholes reported to Florida Department of Environmental Protection, 1964 - 2012

This map plots out the locations of sinkholes reported to the FDEP over a period of 50 years. This does not represent all sinkholes to have formed in the five-county Tampa Bay region during that time; rather, this is just the list of those that have been reported to FDEP, and is therefore geographically biased toward areas with higher populations.
